What Is N3ST3DLOOPS+

N3ST3DLOOPS stands for Nom Nom Nerds' Serious Tech Talks and Tutorials: Delivering Lively Offerings on Programming and Systems

A rather long title resulting in a rather cool acronym. We'll stick with N3ST3DLOOPS from here on.

N3ST3DLOOPS is a study group that currently intends to have 3 forms:

Book Study Group

This is based on the idea of Design Pattern Study Groups that were formed to study the GoF Design Patterns book. See this link to get a feel of how one might be run

We've had good experience in the past with a Design Patterns study group that went on to study other software engineering books.

Elements

  • Classic Tech book
  • "Timeless"
  • Go through whole thing - rare in this day and age
  • Assigned reader's
  • Group discussion
